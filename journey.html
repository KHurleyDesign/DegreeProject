User Journeys

After creating the personas, I analyzed a key issue brought up in the interview and created a user journey to examine before, during, and after the issue. I thought about what the user is doing, why they are doing it, how the user might be thinking or feeling, what is frustrating them, and how we as designers could potentially improve the interaction. These user journeys allowed me to start to think about design improvements through the lens of the user.

  • Robert

Robert

User Goal

Robert wants to be able to connect with family, friends, and old classmates. As well as staying up to date on town news.

Discovery

Roberts extended family recommends Facebook to him. He sees how the app would be useful.

User Goal

Robert wants to stay connected with extended family and friends.

Account Setup

Robert downloads Facebook and sets up an account. Account set up goes quickly and if easy for him.

Friending People

Robert wants to friend his family, extended family, as well as some of his old hunting buddies. Robert is able to find most of the people successfully but has trouble finding some of the people he knows has Facebook.

User Goal

Robert wants to “friend” the people he knows.

Real Friend Request

Robert receives a friend request from someone that he knows from high school. He accepts the friend request.

Thoughts and Feelings

Robert is exited to see what his old friend is up to.

Bot Friend Request

Robert receives a friend request from someone he does not remember but not wanting to offend the person if he does actually know them he accepts the friend request anyway.

Challenges

Not being able to distinguish a real friend request and a bot.

Opportunities for Inovation

Better bot prevention, education about bots and warning when the person is not in your friend group.

Discovery of Bot

Robert asks his daughter a question about the app. The daughter sees the unknown friend and upon further examination, the "friends" have sexual photos attached to the account. The daughter confronts Robert.

Thoughts and Feelings

Robert is angry and embarrassed.

Deleting Account

Robert upset and embarrassed he deletes his account.

Thoughts and Feelings

Robert is frustrated and disapointed with Facebook.

User Goal

Robert does not want to be associated with Facebook anymore.

Martha

User Goal

Martha wants to be able to use facebook to communicate with family that does not live within an easily drivable distance.

Discovery

Martha's daughter mentions to her that she should join Facebook, it would be easier to communicated and they would be able to share videos and photos. Martha is hesitant but she says that she will look into it.

Challenges

Martha feels uncomfortable around technology.

Reaserch

Martha mentions the conversation to her granddaughter who then shows Martha her own account and explains why it might be useful. Martha agrees to sign up.

Challenges

Martha has heard negative things about Facebook, she thinks that it is where a lot of people air out there "dirty laundry".

Thoughts and Feelings

Martha is apprehensive about the process but is willing to try and excited about the possibilities.

Account Setup

Martha and her granddaughter sit down to start the account she does not have an email so they must set one up first. They then go through the setup process and add profile photo/information.

First Comment

Martha wants to comment on a photo of her grandchild that her daughter posted.

Challenges

Martha might not understand how to interact with the comment interface/ if it went through. The language or tone of the comment might not be appropriate causing confusion on her or the viewers part.

Opportunities for Inovation

Prompt that a user could opt into that explains the interface and gives examples of tone/language used.

First Post

Martha wants to post about an outing that she has with her granddaughter to her "friends".

Facebook Messanger

Martha decides that she want to message her daughter through Facebook and is prompted with a new interface for the messaging system.

Henry

User Goal

Henry wants to disseminate information about practice and games to players and parents.

Creation of Separate Page

Henry wants a Separate page for all of this baseball team's information. He creates a community organization page.

Customer Goal

Henry wants a space that he can share information with his players and parents that is separate from his personal Facebook page.

Challenges

Deciding what type of page it is based on Facebook's options.

Sharing the Page

Henry adds his assistant coach the page and invited his players and their parents.

Thinking and Feeling

Henry finds this process tedious and wishes there was a better way to share the page.

Posting an Update

After a heavy rainfall Henry decides to cancel practice to preserve the field. He makes a post about it to the group.

Using the Poll Feature

Henry wants players and parents input on what night they should do the senior celebration. He creates a poll within the group.

Challenges

Getting all the players and parents to vote on the poll.

Opportunities for Innovation

Notification and reminder system for the poll encureging members of the group to participate.

Removing Members

The season has ended and Henry needs to remove past players and parents from the group.
