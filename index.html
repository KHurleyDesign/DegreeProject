Designing Inclusion

According to the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC) One in Four U.S adults - 61 million Americans have a disability. So why is it that designers and developers often overlook web accessibility? This project looks at the Facebook experience for users over 60 to explore accessibility focused design. Through research, interviews, user personas, user journeys, and idiation of ideas this project hopes to promote further thinking and exploration these concepts.
