Bibliography

My research process began in August 2018. I looked for sources as varied as color contrast accessibility, to Google’s accessibility guidelines. Below is a comprehensive list of the sources that I drew inspiration or education from. If you are interested in diving into accessible design, these sources are a good place to start.

  • Books
  • Articles
  • Websites
